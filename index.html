Our Mission

Company History

2020: TravelDiscover was founded with a vision to revolutionize travel planning through personalized recommendations.

2021: Launched our first AI-powered recommendation engine, serving over 10,000 users in the first year.

2022: Expanded our database to include over 50,000 destinations worldwide and introduced real-time local information.

2023: Reached 1 million users and launched our mobile application with enhanced features.

2024: Introduced advanced personalization features and expanded to serve users in over 100 countries.
